Vancouver is an incredible city for photography. The mix of urban landscapes and natural beauty provides endless opportunities.

## Favorite Spots

Some of my favorite places to shoot:

- Stanley Park seawall at sunset
- Granville Island on foggy mornings
- Downtown architecture at night
- North Shore mountains
